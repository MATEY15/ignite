About Ignite

Ignite is a healthcare growth agency that specialises in attracting high-value patients. Whether you’re an orthodontist, plastic surgeon, facial aesthetician or implant dentist, we know how to get you patients.

We work with practices that are tired of waiting for referrals or word of mouth to grow their practice. Practices that are tired of getting burnt and ready to invest in real growth, without compromising on care, value or price. Does that sound like you? Work with us.

How we work

Partnership

We are probably more into you than you into us. We have a buzzer that goes off every time you get a new patient.

Coaching

We don’t just send a flurry of leads to your receptionist. We coach you and your team until you get it right!

Industry insiders

We only work with healthcare businesses. This means we know the industry and your challenges inside-out.

Flexible

No long-term contracts. Work with us on a monthly rolling basis after our initial 3 month honeymoon period.
